Lesedi Charity Organisation

Lesedi Charity Organisation is dedicated to restoring dignity and improving the well-being of young girls in schools by addressing period poverty.
It's mission is to ensure that no girl misses school or feels excluded because she does not have access to sanitary pads. Through pad distribution, education on
menstrual health, and community awareness, Lesedi empowers girls to stay in class with confidence. The Organisation's impact is seen in improved school attendance,
reduced stigma around menstruation, and the creation of a supportive environment where young girls can focus on their education and future

Our Organisation provides:
  • Sustainable solutions to period poverty by distributing sanitary pads
  • Delivery menstrual health education
  • Creating awareness to break the stigma around menstruation

Our work ensures that young girls remain in school, uphold their dignity and are empowered to reach their full potential
