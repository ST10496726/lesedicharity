About Lesedi Charity

Lesedi Organisation is a non-profit initiative founded in 2018 with a mission to support young girls in schools who struggle to access sanitary pads. We aim to restore dignity, improve school attendance, and empower girls by providing essential menstrual health products and education

Our Mission & Vision

Lesedi Charity is fully committed to the vital mission of ensuring that no girl misses out on education simply due to the lack of sanitary pads—a situation that constitutes a grave violation of educational equity and dignity. Our commitment goes beyond mere provision; we strive to actively promote awareness around the pervasive issue of period poverty, thereby restoring dignity to young women and fostering genuine equality within schools across South Africa. By tackling this fundamental barrier, we empower girls to attend school consistently, confidently, and without shame, allowing them to focus entirely on their studies and secure the brighter futures they deserve..

Our vision is to realize a society transformed, one where period poverty is entirely eradicated, ensuring that menstruation is never a source of disadvantage or distress. We aspire to a future where every young girl, regardless of her background or economic situation, has equal access to quality education and essential health resources. Ultimately, we seek to free girls from the unnecessary constraints of her menstrual cycle, allowing them to participate fully and confidently in life, securing the opportunity to reach their full academic, social, and personal potential. This vision drives every program, every donation, and every awareness campaign we undertake.

Our Story

Founded in 2018, Lesedi Charity began as a small community initiative driven by simple acts of kindness to support young girls struggling to access sanitary products. Recognizing the critical link between menstrual hygiene and educational equity, we formalized our operations, and over the years, Lesedi has grown into a registered non-profit organization impacting hundreds of families across Johannesburg. This growth allows us to provide reliable, essential support, demonstrating that our foundation built on those initial small acts has successfully transformed into a sustainable movement creating lasting change and ensuring more girls can stay in school and achieve their full potential..
