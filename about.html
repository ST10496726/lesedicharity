Our Mission

At Lesedi Organisation, our mission is to restore dignity and empower young girls by ensuring access to sanitary products and menstrual health education.
We believe that no girl should miss school or feel excluded simply because of a natural biological process. By addressing period poverty, we aim to break the stigma around
menstruation and create an environment where girls can thrive academically and personally.

Our Story

Founded in 2018, Lesedi Organisation was born out of compassion and a lived reality. One of our founders reflected on her school years, recalling how a peer
struggled to access sanitary pads. That experience illuminated a wider problem faced by many girls—period poverty—and inspired the creation of Lesedi Organisation. Since then, we have been
dedicated to providing practical solutions and long-term support to ensure that every girl has the opportunity to reach her full potential.

Our impact

  • Access to Sanitary Pads: We have distributed thousands of sanitary pads to girls in underserved schools and communities.
  • Menstrual Health Education: We run workshops that provide knowledge, awareness, and confidence to young girls, encouraging open conversations about menstrual health.
  • Improved School Attendance: Through our initiatives, girls no longer have to miss school due to lack of resources, improving their academic performance and future opportunities
  • Breaking the Stigma: By engaging with schools, communities, and parents, we are helping to normalize conversations around menstruation, fostering understanding and support.
