About Lesedi Charity

Lesedi Charity is dedicated to empowering underprivileged communities through education, health, and social support programs. Our work is guided by compassion, integrity, and the belief that every individual deserves a chance to thrive.

Our Mission

To create meaningful opportunities that improve the lives of children and families in need.

Our Vision

A South Africa where every child has access to education and a safe environment to grow.

Our Core Values

  • Compassion: We care deeply about the communities we serve.
  • Integrity: We act with honesty and transparency in all we do.
  • Collaboration: We work together with partners, volunteers, and communities.
  • Empowerment: We help individuals and communities reach their full potential.

Our Story

Founded in 2015, Lesedi Charity began as a small community initiative to support local children’s education. Over the years, we have grown into a registered non-profit organization impacting hundreds of families across Johannesburg and beyond. Our journey has been driven by the belief that small acts of kindness can create lasting change.
