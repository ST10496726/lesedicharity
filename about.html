About Lesedi Charity

Lesedi Organisation is a non-profit initiative founded in 2018 with a mission to support young girls in schools who struggle to access sanitary pads. We aim to restore dignity, improve school attendance, and empower girls by providing essential menstrual health products and education

Our Mission & Vision

To ensure that no girl misses out on education due to a lack of sanitary pads, while promoting awareness, dignity, and equality in schools across South Africa.

A South Africa where every child has access to education and a safe environment to grow.

Our Core Values

  • Compassion: We care deeply about the communities we serve.
  • Integrity: We act with honesty and transparency in all we do.
  • Collaboration: We work together with partners, volunteers, and communities.
  • Empowerment: We help individuals and communities reach their full potential.

Our Story

Founded in 2018, Lesedi Charity began as a small community initiative to support young girls who needed sanitary products. Over the years, we have grown into a registered non-profit organization impacting hundreds of families across Johannesburg. Small acts of kindness built the foundation of lasting change.
