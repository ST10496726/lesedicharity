Get Involved

Join Lesedi Charity in making a genuine and tangible difference in our communities, because the future success of young girls relies directly on the support we provide today. Every form of contribution acts as a vital resource in our fight against period poverty, ensuring that a girl's menstrual cycle never becomes a barrier to her education. Whether you choose to donate funds which directly provide essential dignity packs and sustainable sanitary supplies, volunteer your time to assist with distribution and empowerment workshops, or simply spread awareness of the critical issues surrounding menstrual equity, every single action counts and is instrumental in restoring dignity, improving school attendance, and ultimately, empowering the next generation of female leaders across Johannesburg. Together, we can turn period days back into school days and illuminate the path to a brighter future for all.
