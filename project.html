Our Initiatives and Projects

Sanitary Pad Distribution

We provide free sanitary pads to young girls in schools and underprivileged communities. This ensures that no learner has to miss classes due to lack of menstrual products.

  • 5000 pads distributed since 2018
  • 12 schools reached
  • 6000 girls supported

Menstrual Health Education Workshops

We host interactive sessions where girls learn about menstrual health, hygiene practices, and self-care. These workshops empower them with knowledge and confidence.

  1. Peer-to-peer learning activities
  2. Q&A sessions to break myths and taboos
  3. Distribution of informational booklets

Community Awareness Campaigns

We involve parents, teachers, and community leaders in open conversations to break the stigma around menstruation.

  • School and community talks
  • Radio discussions and outreach programs
  • Partnerships with local leaders
