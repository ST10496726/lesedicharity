Our initiatives and Projects

Sanitary Pad distribution

We provide free sanitary pads to young girls in schools and underprivileged communities. This ensures that no learner
has to miss classes due to lack of menstrual products.

  • 5000 pads distributed since 2018
  • 12 schools reached
  • 6000 girls supported

Menstrual Health Education Workshops

We host interactive sessions where girls learn about menstrual health, hygiene practices, and self-care. These workshops
empower them with knowledge and confidence to manage their periods with dignity.

  1. Peer-to-peer learning activities
  2. Q&A sessions to break myths and taboos
  3. Distribution of informational booklets

Community Awareness Campaigns

To break the stigma around menstruation, we involve parents, teachers, and community leaders in open conversations. By normalizing
discussions, we help build supportive environments for young girls.

  • School and community talks
  • Radio discussions and outreach programs
  • Partnerships with local leaders
Reusable Pads & SKills Training

We support sustainable solutions by training women and girls to make reusable pads. This not only addresses period poverty but also
creates small income opportunities.

  1. Eco-friendly and cost-effective products
  2. Skills transfer and empowerment
  3. Long-term impact for communities
Future Projects
  • Expanding our reach to rural schools across more provinces
  • Partnering with health professionals to include reproductive health education
  • Developing mentorship programs for girls
